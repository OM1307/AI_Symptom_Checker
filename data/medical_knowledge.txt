### Headache
Symptoms: pain in head, pressure, sensitivity to light
Causes: stress, dehydration, migraine
Remedies: rest, hydration, cold compress
Medicines: paracetamol, ibuprofen
Doctor: if persistent >2 days

### Fever
Symptoms: high temperature, chills, sweating
Causes: infection, flu
Remedies: hydration, rest
Medicines: paracetamol
Doctor: if >102°F or 3 days

### Cough
Symptoms: throat irritation, dry/wet cough
Causes: cold, infection
Remedies: honey, warm fluids
Medicines: cough syrup
Doctor: if persistent >1 week
